FAQs

What is the difference between signing up as a team-leader and player?

A team leader has the ability of creating a team as well as the responsibility of paying league’s fee, while a player can request to join a team or get team joining requests.

How can I add a field?

First the user must be registered as a landlord, then he\she can open his profile and click on “Add a Field” button, the button will show a field adding form; once the form is filled, the user must click submit.

Where can I find the league request?

All users can find their league requests in their navigation bar.

How can I update my information?

In the user profile page click “Edit” and update your information directly.

How can I create a team?

The team leader is the only authorized user of creating a team, from the team leader navigation bar click “Teams” button, then click on “create your team” a form will show to fill in, then click “Create” and your team has been created.

Can the player join to the team?

Yes, by sending a joining request to the preferred team.

How can I know about upcoming leagues?

The admin will update the upcoming events and news where all users and visitors can view it in home page.

How can I know the league results?

It will be displayed on the upcoming events section at home page.

From where can I get the team’s points?

On the team’s page the points are displayed above.
